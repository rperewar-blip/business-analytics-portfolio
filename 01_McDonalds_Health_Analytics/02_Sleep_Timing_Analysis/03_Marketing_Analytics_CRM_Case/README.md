Design a data-driven marketing analytics and CRM framework to improve customer engagement and retention.

## Case Context

- Focus on omnichannel customer analytics

## Tools & Technologies

## Methodology
1. Defined marketing KPIs across channels
2. Designed customer segmentation approach
3. Mapped analytics workflows
4. Translated business goals into data requirements

## Key Insights
- Analytics-driven CRM improves personalization
- KPI alignment is critical for omnichannel strategy
- Segmentation supports targeted campaigns
